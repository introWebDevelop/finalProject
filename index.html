What is Social Media?

Social media by definition is the means of interactions among people in which they create, share, and/or exchange information and ideas in virtual communities and networks.

What is mental health?

Mental health is a form of health that includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.
