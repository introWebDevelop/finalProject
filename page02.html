How Social Media affects our mental health?

Now if you didn't already know, social media can affect us in many ways. Shocking, I know. The use of social media significantly impacts mental health. It can enhance connection, increase self-esteem, and improve a sense of belonging. But it can lead to tremendous stress, pressure to compare oneself to others, and increased sadness and isolation, which isn't good. But don't worry, you aren't the only person dealing with this. Research from an experiment performed by the National Institutes of Health, has found that more than 80% of total participants reported frequent exposure to social networks. The results revealed that 48.3% of the participants were found to have depression, 22.6% suffered from anxiety, and 19.4% have both.
